Campaign description
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Brighter Tomorrow Therapy, a licensed Nevada mental-health practice, sends SMS to clients who have opted in. We collect two separate, independent consents: (a) appointment texts — confirmations, reminders, and schedule changes (date and time only, no health information); and (b) marketing texts — occasional practice updates and win-back outreach to clients who have not visited recently. Each consent is captured with its own unchecked checkbox (or its own verbal/paper agreement); marketing consent is optional and is never a condition of service. Recipients opt in via our website booking/contact form, verbally during a call with our staff or AI scheduling assistant, by texting a keyword to our number, or on an in-person intake form. Recipients can reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance at any time.

How do end-users consent to receive messages?  (40–2048 chars) — CRITICAL FIELD
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
End users opt in through any of these methods, and marketing consent is ALWAYS collected separately from appointment consent. (1) Website — on the contact form at https://brightertomorrowtherapy.com/contact (and the booking form), the user enters their mobile number and sees TWO separate unchecked checkboxes immediately on the page. Box A (appointment texts): "I agree to receive appointment reminders, confirmations, and schedule changes from Brighter Tomorrow Therapy at the number provided. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help." Box B (marketing texts, optional): "I separately agree to receive marketing and practice-update texts from Brighter Tomorrow Therapy. This is optional and not a condition of service. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help." Neither box is pre-checked. (2) Verbally — during a call with our staff or AI scheduling assistant, the caller is read the appointment disclosure and, as a separate question, the optional marketing disclosure; each agreement is logged independently. (3) By text — the user texts a keyword (START, JOIN, SUBSCRIBE) to our number. (4) In person — on our paper intake form, which has the same two separate consent checkboxes. Mobile opt-in data and consent are never shared with third parties or sold. See https://brightertomorrowtherapy.com/privacy-policy.
