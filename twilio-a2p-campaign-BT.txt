Campaign description
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Brighter Tomorrow Therapy, a licensed Nevada mental-health practice, sends SMS to existing and prospective clients who have opted in. Messages include appointment confirmations, reminders, and schedule changes (date and time only — no health information), plus occasional practice updates and win-back outreach to clients who have not visited recently. Recipients opt in via a consent checkbox on our website booking/contact form, verbally during a phone call with our staff or AI scheduling assistant, by texting a keyword to our number, or on an in-person intake form. Recipients can reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance at any time.

How do end-users consent to receive messages?  (40–2048 chars) — CRITICAL FIELD
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
End users opt in to receive text messages from Brighter Tomorrow Therapy through any of these methods: (1) Website — while booking an appointment or submitting the contact form at https://brightertomorrowtherapy.com, the user enters their mobile number and checks an unchecked consent box that reads: "By checking this box, I agree to receive appointment reminders and marketing text messages from Brighter Tomorrow Therapy at the number provided. Consent is not a condition of service. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help." (2) Verbally — during a phone call with our staff or AI scheduling assistant, the caller is read the same disclosure and verbally agrees; the consent is logged to their record. (3) By text — the user texts a keyword such as START, JOIN, or SUBSCRIBE to our practice number. (4) In person — on our paper intake form at the office. Mobile opt-in data and consent are never shared with third parties or sold. Opt-in and opt-out are also described in our privacy policy: https://brightertomorrowtherapy.com/privacy-policy.
